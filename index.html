I am an Assistant Professor in the Economics Department at Bentley University in Waltham, Massachusetts.
My primary research interest is Health Economics. I have also worked on topics in Industrial Organization and Information Economics.
I received my PhD from The Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and my BA, magna cum laude, from Cornell University. Prior to graduate school, I worked at the Federal Trade Commission's Bureau of Economics.
